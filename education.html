Key Research Papers

Rough Volatility2018

Volatility is Rough

Gatheral, Jaisson, Rosenbaum · Quantitative Finance

The foundational paper establishing log-volatility as a fractional Brownian motion with H ≈ 0.1. Direct basis for the Volterra co-authored research paper.

Deep Hedging2019

Deep Hedging

Buehler, Gonon, Teichmann, Wood · Mathematical Finance

Recasting derivative hedging as a reinforcement learning problem — the bridge between RAMPA's PPO agent and classical delta-hedging theory.

Transformers2017

Attention Is All You Need

Vaswani et al. · NeurIPS 2017

The architecture that redefined sequence modeling — attention mechanism and positional encoding studied for financial time-series applications.

Policy Gradient2017

Proximal Policy Optimization Algorithms

Schulman, Wolski, Dhariwal, Radford, Klimov · OpenAI

The clipped surrogate objective and stability advantages over TRPO. Directly implemented in RAMPA's portfolio allocation layer.

Neural Rough Vol2019

Neural Network Calibration of rough Bergomi

Bayer, Horvath, Muguruza, Stemper, Tomas

Training DNNs to calibrate rough volatility at inference speed — the theoretical basis for RAMPA's millisecond-latency Bergomi calibrator.

Pricing Theory1973

The Pricing of Options and Corporate Liabilities

Black & Scholes · Journal of Political Economy

The 1973 paper that created quantitative finance. Read the original derivation — the heat equation transformation is still the clearest exposition of risk-neutral pricing.

Term Structure1985

A Theory of the Term Structure of Interest Rates

Cox, Ingersoll, Ross · Econometrica

The CIR model — mean-reversion, non-negative rates, and the square-root diffusion. Foundational context for the Columbia Financial Engineering curriculum.

Hawkes Processes1971

Spectra of Some Self-Exciting and Mutually Exciting Point Processes

Alan Hawkes · Biometrika

The original Hawkes process — the self-exciting structure underlying both the HFT order flow model and the Volterra paper's jump-diffusion component.

Backtesting2016

The Deflated Sharpe Ratio

Bailey & López de Prado · Journal of Portfolio Management

Correcting for backtest overfitting and multiple hypothesis testing. Applied in every strategy evaluation to prevent reporting inflated Sharpe ratios.

Microstructure2016

Market Microstructure Invariance

Kyle & Obizhaeva · Econometrica

Trade size, price impact, and market activity invariance. Context for the Hawkes-based HFT execution design and adverse selection modeling.

Vol Asymptotics2004

Moment Explosions and Long-Term Behavior of Affine Stochastic Volatility Models

Andersen & Piterbarg · Mathematical Finance

Moment explosions in Heston and other affine models — essential for understanding where FFT Fourier pricing engines fail and why dampening parameters matter.

Rough Vol Sim2020

Efficient Simulation of the Rough Bergomi Model

Bennedsen, Lunde, Pakkanen · Quantitative Finance

Hybrid simulation scheme for rough Bergomi combining exact fractional Brownian motion increments with Euler discretization. Implemented in the Volterra arbitrage engine.
